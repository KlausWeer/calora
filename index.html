Twenty-five years
in the making ↘

Our workshop in Galle spent a quarter of a century making fine silver for a single European house — every piece bound for a name that wasn't ours. When that door closed, what remained was the craft itself.

Calora is that craft, finally under its own name — and finally, yours to wear.
